About SIWS College

Our legacy, vision, and mission

SIWS College

SIWS College, established in 1969 in Wadala, Mumbai, has been empowering students with quality education for decades. The college offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in science, commerce, and arts.

Our mission is to nurture academic excellence, holistic development, and social responsibility among students. With experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, and a vibrant campus life, SIWS continues to be a leading institution in Mumbai.
